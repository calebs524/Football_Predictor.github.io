How do Predictions Work in Football?

Predicting the game of football is a very volatile activity. Anything can happen: injuries, cuts, trades, coaches getting fired. It's hard to make predictions on games, a team's season, and even who wins the superbowl. For example, no one predicted the Commanders getting to the NFC champtionship in 2025, but it happened. In this section, I provide my own "predictions" for the 2025 season. Some will be based on teams, some based on players, but everything is my own opinion. I don't pretend to know everything, because I don't. All I can do is tell you what I think "could" happen.
